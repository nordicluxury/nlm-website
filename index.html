About Nordic Luxury Media

Independent media,
Nordic roots,
global audience.

Nordic Luxury Media is an independent media brand based in Helsinki. We produce content across real estate, finance, geopolitics, architecture, and culture — distributed on TikTok, YouTube, Instagram, and Threads.

We are building the media company that the next generation actually deserves to read.

Real estate is our home vertical. Our property channels cover the Finnish market in depth — from Helsinki apartments to regional homes — and have built a following of buyers, renters, and anyone genuinely interested in where and how people live in Finland.

Beyond real estate, our Global and Europe channels cover markets, rankings, and geopolitical analysis for an audience that spans continents. TikTok alone drives over 12 million views per year. We are building this for the long term — more channels, more verticals, one editorial standard that does not cut corners.
